    Next direct ferry (Duration 2.5 hr) at 3:30 PM Cost: $100

  • Hawaii One Hyperloop:
    Kaneho->Honolulu by road taxi (Duration 20 min) :: Next Hyperloop (Duration 10 min) at 10:30 AM to Hana Other hyperloops available at 2:30 PM, 5:30 PM. Cost: $10+$500

Design explorations

Search Results

That's first thing that Leia will see after entering the journey details -

Search results will have the following entities:

  • Sort and Filter

  • Search results

  • Coronavirus guideline notice

  • Travel options: App provides 4 travel mediums(Flight, Super Ferry, Seaplane and Hyperloop) that Leia can choose for her journey.

  • Result page: Time, Duration, Locations, Airlines name, Airline type, Price. Maybe Tags like: Recommended, Cheapest, Fastest, etc. Leia can sort those things in the sort and filter page.

  • Free cancellation message block: To help Leia see only free cancellations commute options in case of change in travel plans due to coronavirus or some other reasons.

Modify Search Results

In case Leia have to change plans(Change in Date, No. of travellers, Cabin class, etc) then she will see a panel with modification entities.

The entry point for the modify results panel is in the top right corner of the search results page (Edit).

Sort and Filter

Sorting and filtering will help Leia to search for a specific commute option available.

Both sorting and filtering will take place in a single page. Leia can swithch b/w sort and filter by clicking on the tabs. Changes need to be saved in order to take place.

Leia can apply multiple filters while in the case Sorting only single entity can be selected (Either fastest flight/ cheapest).

Filter categories:

  • No of stops: Non-Stop, etc.

  • Departure time: Early morning, Morning, Afternoon, Evening and Night.

  • Airline: Multiple airline options. Such as, Hawaii Airlines, Westjet, Allegiant Airlines, etc.

More Information/Detailed view page

Detailed view will display all the breakdowns with details, such as cabs and flight, Along with fare details and booking option.
