PETDOO

Premium pet adoption platform that specializes in helping a pet lover find a pet that matches their lifestyle.

Primary Goals -

The primary goal is to help find a pet lover a perfect pet that matches their lifestyles.



Secondary Goals -

  • Browse pets by - Shelters, verified breeders, pet homes, etc. & various factors.

  • Search a specific pet, breed, shelter, etc.

  • Bookmark & share a pet.

  • Booking an appointment.

  • Create a post for pet adoption. Log in as a pet house.

  • Make donations.



Target audience -

  • Pet finders

  • Shelters, Pet homes, etc.



Pain points -

  • Unclear process of adoption

  • Lack of information from shelters about pets and the adoption process.

  • Choose the perfect pet - Not able to compare between 2 pets

  • Physical registration & vaccination



Ideas -

  • Survey on registration. This is skippable and can be done later in the profile section.

  • Giving an elastic search kind of filter for the user to find a more specific pet they might be looking for.

  • Making the adoption process simpler by getting all the legal & required docs/details virtually.

  • Selling a pet for adoption is also a part of the app and allows the user to make a pet available for adoption.

  • Instant messaging to the shelters/pet houses for more specific information.

  • Donations to the active rescue camps - This might use a third party payment systems like Razorpay.



Assumptions -

  • Users of this app are genuine and not using this platform to hurt animals & selling animals further in a pricey range.

  • During the time of making an appointment/booking a pet, the user needs to provide govt. ID's for manual verification of the users to neglect the first point.

  • Users cannot post about wild animals.

  • Shelters/Pet houses have a nicely filled stuff to give information to the user instantly.

Onboarding & Surveys -

Onboarding:

The onboarding process is super quick resistance free. As soon as the user lands on the registration/login page he can quickly connect via social logins.

Users can also continue as a guest and do the onboarding later.

Surveys:

This allows us to understand the user better - Their needs, lifestyles, what they like, etc.

This will help to find a suitable pet for the user.

Users can also skip the surveys in between the progress as I don't want to add resistance. They can fill out the survey later in the process.

Homepage

Once the onboarding and survey are done / the user decides to continue as a guest he will land on the homepage.

If the user has filled out the survey he will see the top matches and the % of a match on a pet card/profile. Which gives him more accurate data about the pet & helps him to decide which pet to choose.

Assumption: If the user has not filled out the survey/continued as a guest then there should be an option to motivate the user to fill out the survey. Something like an upsell-block telling - Find a pet that matches your lifestyle. On click of that upsell block, he will hand on the survey page/particular question where he left.

Individual category page

The individual page will show results related to a particular topic like - Near you, Trending, Most viewed, etc

Users can also wishlist the pets they liked throughout the category and check back on later. This allows them to compare the pets easily.

Search & Filter

Let's Imagine I am looking for a pet for my friend and her birthday is coming in 2 days. I don't want to go through the full list of pets. I have the pet picture in my mind(Breed, age, hair, etc.) that I wish to adopt. So In that case I can search for pets by location & then add up some filters to check out the options or I can search for a specific pet only.

Pet/Shelter page & Adoption.

Page when I tap/click on a pet/shelter.

This page contains All the information about the pet & shelter. Opening time, Breed, Other pets in that shelter, Pet age, etc.

Users can get more information about the pet here by messaging the shelter (Vaccination & Documents info.). Also, Users can book an appointment/book a pet for a visit.

The adoption process requires some basic information Like Name, Email, Number, etc. Logged in users do not need to enter that data until and unless they are adopting a pet for a friend. Once that is done you can just pick out a date and time slot that suits you the best and confirm the appointment
