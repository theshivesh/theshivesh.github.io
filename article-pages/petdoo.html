Petdoo - Connecting Hearts, Paws, and Homes

Petdoo is a comprehensive platform designed to bridge the gap between pet lovers and their perfect companions. With a user-friendly interface and innovative features, we streamline the adoption process, empower shelters and pet homes, and ensure every pet finds its forever home. From simplified registration to instant communication with shelters, PetFinder is your trusted companion in the journey of pet adoption.

  • My role

    Research & product strategy, user testings, user flows, interaction, visual design

  • Team members

    It's a fun project so just me :)

  • Timeline

    1 Week

Primary Goal:

Our primary goal is to facilitate pet lovers in finding their perfect furry companion that seamlessly fits into their lifestyle.

Secondary Goals:

  • Browse pets from various sources such as shelters, verified breeders, and loving pet homes.

  • Search for specific pets, breeds, or shelters with ease.

  • Bookmark and share beloved pets within the community.

  • Schedule appointments for pet visits or adoption.

  • Enable pet adoption posts for shelters and pet homes.

  • Facilitate donations to support active rescue efforts.

Target Audience:

  • Pet Enthusiasts: Individuals seeking to add a new member to their family.

  • Shelters and Pet Homes: Organizations dedicated to finding loving homes for animals in need.

Pain Points Addressed:

  • Complex Adoption Processes: We simplify the adoption journey, providing clear guidance and assistance.

  • Lack of Information: Our platform ensures shelters provide comprehensive details about pets and the adoption process.

  • Decision Making: Users can compare different pets to find their perfect match easily.

  • Administrative Hassles: We streamline registration, vaccination, and other legal requirements digitally.

Ideas:

  • Registration Survey: A skippable survey streamlines registration, ensuring a smooth onboarding process.

  • Elastic Search Filters: Users can refine their search criteria for more tailored pet suggestions.

  • Virtual Adoption Process: All necessary documentation and details are handled digitally, simplifying the adoption process.

  • Pet Adoption Listings: Users can list pets for adoption, fostering a community-driven approach to finding homes for animals in need.

  • Instant Messaging: Seamless communication with shelters and pet homes for quick and specific inquiries.

  • Donation Integration: Support rescue efforts by contributing through secure third-party payment systems like Razorpay.

Assumptions:

  • User Integrity: We trust our users to genuinely seek companionship for pets, not exploitation or profit.

  • Verification Process: Government ID verification ensures accountability and safeguards animal welfare.

  • Responsible Posting: Users are restricted from posting about wild animals, maintaining the focus on domestic pet adoption.

  • Information Availability: Shelters and pet homes are equipped to provide timely and detailed information to potential adopters.
